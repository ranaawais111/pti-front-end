PAKISTAN Tehreek-e-Insaf

The Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI; Urdu: پاکستان تحريکِ انصاف or پی ٹی‌‌آئی, lit.('Pakistan Movement for Justice') is a centrist political party in Pakistan. It was founded in 1996 by the Pakistani cricketer-turned-politician IMRAN KHAN.

IMRAN KHAN INSTA ACCOUNT The PTI is one of the three major Pakistani political parties alongside the Pakistan Muslim League–Nawaz (PML–N) and the Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP), and it is the largest party in terms of representation in the National Assembly since the 2018 Pakistani general election. With over 10 million members in Pakistan and abroad, it claims to be Pakistan's largest party by primary membership as well as one of the largest political parties in the world.
